Teacher Notes — Ms. Rodriguez, Algebra I

Unit pacing adjustments from last year:
- Unit 2 (Linear Equations) needed an extra week for struggling students.
  Added a review day before the checkpoint.
- Unit 5 (Linear Systems) was tight — moved one application day to the
  following semester review.

STAAR focus:
- Emphasize A1.5C (applications) — this shows up heavily on STAAR.
- A1.7C (quadratic formula) is tested but not a major weight on EOC.
- Students consistently struggle with A1.6B (factoring trinomials).

Resources:
- We use the district-provided textbook (Algebra I, McGraw-Hill, 2023 edition).
- Supplemental: Khan Academy for remediation, Desmos for graphing activities.

Class structure:
- 90-minute block schedule, every other day (A/B days).
- 28 students enrolled.

Known gaps:
- This syllabus doesn't cover exponential functions at all. Our feeder middle
  school covers basic exponents, but not exponential growth/decay.
- Data analysis unit is underweighted — STAAR has been increasing emphasis.
